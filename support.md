# InnerRing Support

**Last Updated: July 19, 2026**

Need help with InnerRing? Email [innerring.app@gmail.com](mailto:innerring.app@gmail.com) with a short description of the problem and the iOS version you are using.

We aim to reply within 3 business days. Safety concerns involving immediate danger should be reported to local emergency services first.

## Account Access

InnerRing accounts use a recovery key instead of an email address or phone number. We cannot recover or replace a lost recovery key.

Never send your recovery key to us. InnerRing support will not ask for it by email, in a message, or through an external link.

## Messages, Contacts, and Subscriptions

Before contacting support:

- Check that InnerRing is up to date in the App Store.
- Check your internet connection and try reopening the app.
- For subscription or billing issues, review your Apple subscriptions or contact Apple Support. Payments and refunds are handled by Apple.
- For identity-sensitive account requests, be prepared to verify control through the signed-in app. Email alone is not proof of account ownership.

## Safety and Privacy

Use the in-app reporting tools to report a message or display name. They provide the relevant content and account context more safely than email.

See [Safety at InnerRing](safety.html) for reporting, blocking, complaints, and urgent-help information. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.html) for privacy rights and data requests.

## Contact

**Email**: [innerring.app@gmail.com](mailto:innerring.app@gmail.com)

When contacting us, include only the information needed to investigate the issue. Do not include your recovery key or unnecessary sensitive information.
